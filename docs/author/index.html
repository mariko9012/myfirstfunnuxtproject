About the author

Hi! I'm Mariko, a city enthusiast currently traveling around the world. I'm a researcher, editor and writer on the urban issues, and I have been involved in the various international urban programs and initiatives all over the world.

As an Urbanist, I'm most passionate about our cities. I’ve involved in many international educational projects like 4CITIES, DIVERCITIES, and have visited more than 120 organizations and 50 cities accross the globe for research. More specifically I’m interested in ethno-cultural diversity in our cities, and the notion of nationality and sense of belonging.

As a project manager, Having worked in a design firm Loftwork as a director and marketer, I organize various events and workshops in collaboration with the international organizations, such as 2 days workshop Global Goals Jam and Social Good Summit Tokyo, or more urbanism related events like subjective map workshop and urbanist meetup.

As a writer / translator, I work across the spectrum of urban design, urbanism and architecture. For the past 6 years, I’ve conducted various in depth field research/interviews across the world and have written the analytical articles and papers as a freelance writer.

Personally, I'm a huge traveler – I’ve lived in ten different cities, in seven different countries. I’m always passionate about making my homes everywhere in the world!

If you're interested in working together, or just to have a cup of coffee to know what I’m up to, please email me or check out my LinkedIn page!
