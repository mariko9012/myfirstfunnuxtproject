Organization, initiative

  1. BMW Guggenheim Lab is an ‘traveling’ urban think tank/laboratory and community center. Unfortunately it’s not active anymore, but this idea of having an mobile laboratory focusing on urban life inspires me so much!

  • youarethecity is an urban research, design and planning practice. I like thier bottom-up approach arguing that everybody has agency in designing cities and urban space.

  • Van Alen Institute is an organization that believes design can transform cities, landscapes, and regions to improve people’s lives. Their projects and website are full of inspiration.

  • The center for urban pedagogy is a nonprofit organization that uses the power of design and art to increase meaningful civic engagement.
