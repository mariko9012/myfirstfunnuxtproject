Things to do and read for urbanists @Tokyo

Intro・ Art Center Ongoing, Tokyo ・ Jun 1, 2018


Photo by Andre Benz on Unsplash

Things to do, places/project to check out

  • Participate URBANING_U which is a two day camp, that let you learn more about Tokyo thorough urbanist's perspective.

  • If you're an cyclist and want to experience biking in Tokyo, I absolutely recommend TOKYO BY BIKE — this is a very helpful introduction to Japan's bike scene.

  • I love the art center ongoing, which is a special art space / gallery that is very accessible to any artists, including un-famous, fledgling ones.

Read

  • Follow BeingTokyo, which is a rotation curation account on Twitter.

  • Check out the books by Flick Studio, which is a publishing company specialized in Architecture and urban design.

  • I like watching NHK's project Tokyo Miracle City — fragments of everyday lives by Tokyoists.

Organization

  • mi-rimeter is a great team that practice bottom-up urban intervention and tactical urbanism. They also have a design farm

  • Shibuya Hack Project believes in the citizen's power and right to cities.

  • Urbanist Meetup is an online community of people who love city, architecture, urban design, real estate and community development.

  • If you love gardening, you'd love Tokyo Green Space andthis facebook community Urban Farming Tokyo
