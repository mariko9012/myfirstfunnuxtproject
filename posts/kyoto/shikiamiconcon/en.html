I wrote an article on World Architecture Community about SHIKIAMI CONCON, a building complex consisting of a combination of three traditional Japanese terrace houses and 19 shipping containers. I used to think that there's nothing unexplored in the genre of container architecture, but a combination of a Machiya (traditional Japanese wooden houses) and containers was totally a surprise.
