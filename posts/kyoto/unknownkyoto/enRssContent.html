I wrote an article on World Architecture Community about UNKNOWN KYOTO, a newly renovated multi-purpose building including a guesthouse, restaurant, coworking-space standing on a former red-light district of Kyoto. The existing Yūkaku-architecture was beautifully renovated by expo — I recommend all the architecture lovers to give a visit!
