I wrote an article on World Architecture Community about Chidori-Bunka — a community centre, art space, a shop and bar, located at the edge of the second biggest city in Japan, Osaka. Renovated by dot architects, this places is full of love and respect toward ordinary buildings and amateur carpentry.
